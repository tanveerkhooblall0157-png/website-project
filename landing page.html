"Your Dream, Our Design"

lunova decor is a Mauritian company specializing in providing high-quality decoration services for various occasions, including weddings, birthdays, corporate events, religious ceremonies, and holiday parties. With a focus on transforming venues into elegant and memorable spaces, lunova decor offers customizable decoration packages to suit any budget and event size. The company prides itself on delivering creative, aesthetically pleasing, and detailed décor, ensuring every event is uniquely tailored to the client’s vision. Whether it’s an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, Seem Event brings style and sophistication to every occasion.
